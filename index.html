About

Hi! Here's a little about me:

I'm a New Jersey native, born and raised in South Brunswick. I moved to New Hampshire to study at Dartmouth College, where I majored in both economics and anthropology. After undergrad, I returned to New Jersey to work at Bloomberg Global Data for an additional year, until I enrolled in the Flatiron School's data science bootcamp, where I learned a lot about machine learning, statistics, and programming in Python and SQL.

In my free time, I love reading and writing fantasy fiction, struggling with the NYT crossword, and rock climbing with friends.

I also occasionally draw and paint. The portrait above is a charcoal sketch I recently did of my mom (who thinks I should've drawn her with fewer wrinkles)!
